Alcohol distillery
Opening 2024 04
The project focuses on the production of premium fruit brandies and gins with a daily capacity up to 400 litres of alcohol. The production efficiently process all kinds of fruits and various herbs. In Ghana, more than 50 % of its fruit production goes to waste. In addition, 85 % of the ethanol (needed for the production of alcoholic beverages) is imported. Our top quality technology enables production from local sources.
